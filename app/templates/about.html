About UiTM Machang Job Recommender

Our Mission

The UiTM Machang Job Recommender System is designed to help graduates from UiTM Machang find suitable job opportunities that match their skills, qualifications, and career goals.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between graduates and employers by providing a platform that uses content-based filtering to make personalized job recommendations. This approach helps graduates find relevant job opportunities while saving time and effort in their job search.

Key Features

  • Personalized job recommendations
  • Skills-based matching
  • Easy application process
  • Career profile management
  • Application tracking

How It Works

Step 1: Create Your Profile

Register and build your profile with your skills, qualifications, education, and career preferences. The more details you provide, the better your recommendations will be.

Step 2: Algorithm Match

Our content-based filtering algorithm analyzes your profile and compares it with available job openings to identify the best matches based on your skills and requirements.

Step 3: Apply & Track

Browse your personalized job recommendations, apply directly through our platform, and track your application status in real-time. Get notified when there are updates.

About UiTM Machang

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Machang is a leading educational institution in Kelantan, Malaysia. Our graduates come from various disciplines including business, technology, humanities, and sciences. The Job Recommender System is part of our commitment to support our graduates' transition into the workforce and enhance their career prospects.
